About Us

Matthew Bach:
Owner & Master Tech

Bachmobile German Auto Service was built around one principle: your German vehicle deserves a specialist. Matthew, the owner and sole technician, has spent years mastering the engineering, electronics, and performance systems that set BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, VW, and Porsche apart from everything else on the road.

When you bring your car to Bachmobile, you work directly with Matthew. No hand-offs, no junior techs. Just the owner himself, giving your vehicle the same attention he would give his own. That personal accountability is exactly why customers keep coming back year after year.

Located on Farm to Market 2920 in Tomball, Bachmobile serves drivers across the greater Houston area who refuse to settle for anything less than expert care for their European vehicle.
